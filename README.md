# Task 2: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objective

The goal of this project was to explore the dataset to identify patterns, trends, and distributions using Microsoft Excel. The analysis focused on sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and order trends to generate actionable business insights.

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Excel Functions (AVERAGE, COUNT, SUM, MEDIAN)

## Analysis Performed

### Basic Statistics

* Calculated total revenue.
* Calculated the average order value.
* Determined the median order value.
* Counted the total number of orders.

### Trend Analysis

* Identified the highest sales month and year.
* Analyzed whether sales were increasing or decreasing over time.
* Compared revenue across different years.

### Product Analysis

* Identified the best-selling product by quantity.
* Identified the product that generated the highest revenue.
* Calculated the average selling price for each product.

### Customer & Order Analysis

* Determined the most frequently used payment method.
* Identified the most common referral source.
* Analyzed coupon code usage.
* Compared customers who used coupons versus those who did not.
* Identified the most common order status.

### Outlier Analysis

* Identified the top 10 highest-value orders.
* Identified products with unusually high unit prices.
* Examined unusually large order quantities.

## Key Findings

* Total revenue: ₦1,264,762
* Chair was the best-selling product by quantity and revenue.
* FreeShip was the most frequently used coupon.
* 2023 recorded the highest sales.
* Online was the most frequently used payment method.
* Cancelled orders occurred most frequently.
* Sales fluctuated over time rather than following a consistent upward trend.

## Business Insights

* Chairs generated the highest sales due to strong customer demand despite having a lower average selling price.
* The high cancellation rate suggests the need to investigate operational or customer-related issues.
* Free shipping promotions appear to encourage more purchases.
* Instagram and online payment channels show strong customer engagement and should be considered in future marketing strategies.
